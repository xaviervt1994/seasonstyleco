Shipping & Returns

We want your seasonal picks to arrive safely and on time. Below is a simple overview of how shipping, delivery, and returns work at Season Style Co.

Back to Shop

Policies can be updated over time—always check this page for the latest version.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment is confirmed. During peak seasonal drops or holidays, processing times may be slightly longer.

Estimated Delivery

Most orders arrive within 7–15 business days, depending on your location and the specific item. Some products may ship separately if they come from different partner warehouses.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link (when available). Use that link to follow your package as it makes its way to you.

Returns & Exchanges

If something doesn’t fit or arrives not as expected, you can generally request a return or exchange within 30 days of delivery, as long as the item is unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, certain items (such as undergarments or opened personal-care items) may not be returnable. Any non-returnable items will be clearly noted on the product page.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the item and packaging. We’ll work with you to replace or refund the item as quickly as possible.

How to Start a Return

To begin a return or exchange, email us at support@seasonstyleco.com (update this to your real email) with your order number, the item you’d like to return, and the reason. We’ll reply with the next steps.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we’ll notify you if your refund is approved. Approved refunds are issued back to your original payment method. Banks or card providers may take a few business days to post the refund.
